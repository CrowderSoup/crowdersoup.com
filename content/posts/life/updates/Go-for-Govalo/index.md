This blog post has been a long time coming. I started working a new gig back in Nov. of 2021.

glasses come off for me.

but it wasn't long before I changed roles to Lead Engineer. I have been the most senior engineer
on more than one team, but this is the first time I've actually **lead** a team. I want to share
with you some of the lessons I've learned early on in this role.
